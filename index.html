Quickly estimate mortgage payment, monthly cash flow, annual cash flow, and cash-on-cash return.

How this rental property calculator works

This tool estimates your monthly mortgage payment (principal + interest), adjusts rent for a vacancy rate, and calculates monthly cash flow, annual cash flow, and cash-on-cash return.

What is cash-on-cash return?

Cash-on-cash return compares your annual cash flow to the cash you invested (typically your down payment). It helps investors compare deals quickly.

Frequently asked questions

Does this include taxes, insurance, or repairs?

Include those in your “Monthly Operating Expenses” number. Many investors estimate repairs, maintenance, and management too.

Does this include closing costs?

Not yet. For a conservative estimate, you can add closing costs into the down payment field (or we can add a closing-cost input later).

Does this include appreciation?

This calculator focuses on cash flow and cash-on-cash return. Appreciation and principal paydown can be added later as an “advanced” view.
